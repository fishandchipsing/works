Airpop — Xiaomi Smart Mask

Strategy and experience design for a connected air wearable.

Airpop is a pioneering company in air wearables and respiratory health. Working with their team in 2016–2017, the project focused on digital strategy and user experience for connected, sensing masks — products that do more than filter air.

The interaction model for the Active Mask was built around four conceptual layers: Context (tracking breathing patterns across the city), Shell (device connection and status), Filter (real-time filter condition), and Breathing (live respiratory rhythm visualisation).

The result was a product that turned a passive medical object into an active health companion — released in early 2018 as part of Xiaomi's ecosystem.
