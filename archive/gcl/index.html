Google Creative Lab

Future home AI devices — concepts and interactions.

A collaboration with Google Creative Lab and the Magenta team in New York City, working with Amit Pitaru, Kory Mathewson, and Patrick Hebron.

The project explored new concepts and interactions for intelligent home products — specifically rejecting the dominant metaphor of the human assistant. The goal was to imagine AI that felt neither anthropomorphic nor mechanical: embedded in small, local hardware with behaviours that emerged from context rather than commands.

Confidential project.
