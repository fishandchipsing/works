Teacher of Algorithms

Future jobs as a by-product of learning devices.

As machine learning systems become embedded in everyday products, someone has to teach them. Teacher of Algorithms speculates on a near-future labour market where training AI is a new kind of job — and asks who that work falls to.

The project imagines a product ecosystem that generates micro-tasks: labelling, correcting, demonstrating. These tasks are performed by users who may not realise they are doing them. It raises questions about invisible labour, algorithmic dependency, and who benefits from the data we produce.
