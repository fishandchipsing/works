oio

A hybrid studio of humans and machines building products for a less boring future.

oio is a design and futures studio co-founded with a small team of humans and some machines. We build products and concepts for companies like Google, IKEA, Logitech, and Arduino — and try to make futures that are less predictable than the ones everyone else seems to be designing.

The studio runs partly as a client practice, partly as a research lab, and partly as oio.school — a programme where participants work alongside the studio on future-facing product ideas.
